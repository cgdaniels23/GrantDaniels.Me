Revenue Intelligence Liquid Cosmos 2024–Present

Liquid Cosmos — Revenue Intelligence Suite

Most companies can't tell you which blog post sourced their best client. Liquid Cosmos can. It's a revenue intelligence suite — Calendar, CRM, CMS, and Messaging — built to close the loop between content creation and closed revenue, with AI agents surfacing the signal at every step.

The core problem it solves

Marketing and sales have always operated as separate systems. Content goes out, leads come in, deals close — but the thread between them snaps somewhere in the middle. Liquid Cosmos is built to keep that thread intact: data flows top-to-bottom-funnel, every deal traces back to a content touchpoint, and AI agents sit at the seams to surface what needs optimizing before the next meeting.

Revenue intelligence capabilities

  • First/last/multi-touch attribution per closed deal — know exactly which content sourced each client
  • Time-to-close by acquisition source — identify which content drives faster conversions
  • Topic clusters correlated to deal size — invest in the content that closes bigger
  • Content gap mapping against pipeline drop-off — find the holes before they cost you
  • Cohort LTV by content source — surface which clients stay longest and spend most
  • AI optimization loop — agents flag what to publish, update, or kill before your next revenue review

Messaging Platform Cosmos Labs 2025–Present

Cosmos Comm

Inter-office communications for teams that want fast, clean, and secure messaging without handing their conversations over to a third-party server. Built to be lightweight, self-deployable, and distraction-minimal.

The problem it solves

Most team messaging platforms are either bloated with features nobody uses, tied to cloud infrastructure you don't control, or both. Cosmos Comm is the opposite — fast, focused, and deployable on your own stack in under an hour.

Key design decisions

  • Real-time messaging via WebSockets — no polling, no lag
  • Self-hosted — deploy on your own server or NAS
  • Secure by design — messages stay on your infrastructure
  • Minimal UI — built for focus, not feature discovery

Intelligence Hub Liquid Cosmos 2025–Present

Cosmos Calendar

The center of the Liquid Cosmos universe. Every AI agent, every revenue signal, every attribution data point orbits the calendar — because that's where decisions actually get made. Walk into any meeting already knowing what content launched, how it's converting, and what the AI says to do about it.

Why Calendar is the hub

Most business software generates intelligence that arrives too late, in the wrong context. Cosmos Calendar inverts that: agents surface deal context, content performance data, and optimization recommendations directly into the time block where you need them — before the meeting, not after it. The calendar isn't just a scheduler. It's the decision layer.

What agents surface into it

  • Pre-meeting briefs — relationship history, open deals, last touchpoints, content that influenced the prospect
  • Content performance snapshots — what went live, how it's converting, what the attribution data says
  • Pipeline alerts — deals that have gone quiet, next best actions, revenue gap warnings
  • Optimization signals — which content to update, amplify, or kill based on closed-revenue correlation
  • Cohort intelligence — LTV and conversion patterns by acquisition source, surfaced before strategy calls

Web Application Personal Project 2024

Astrophotography Dashboard

A sky-tracking and dark sky conditions web app built for astrophotographers. Pulls real-time atmospheric data, moon phase, light pollution overlays, and optimal imaging windows into a single dashboard — no more juggling five different apps on a clear night.

What it does

Real-time dark sky condition scoring, moon phase and rise/set calculations, atmospheric seeing and transparency forecasts, and a customizable target planner for deep-sky objects. Designed for use in the field — mobile-first, low-light optimized UI.

Notable features

  • Dark sky index from multiple weather API sources
  • Moon interference calculator per DSO target
  • Apple Watch integration for quick condition checks
  • Low-light UI mode for field use

Fitness PWA Personal Project 2026

Gym Daddy

A dead-simple workout tracker for people who just want to keep their numbers straight. Log your sets, watch your lifts trend up over time, know you're actually on track. No paper notebook. No clutter. No learning curve.

What it does

Open it, tap in your set, close it. Look back at what you hit last week, last month, last quarter — all right there. The whole app is built around the idea that if logging your workout takes longer than doing it, nobody's going to keep doing it.

Why it's a no-brainer

  • Replaces the spiral notebook you've been dragging around
  • Log a full set in three taps — no fumbling, no keyboards
  • Your numbers, over time, so you can actually see progress
  • Lives on your home screen like any other app
  • Private and yours — no account, no ads, no data leaving your phone
